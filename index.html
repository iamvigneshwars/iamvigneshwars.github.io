Skim Unstructured Medical Abstracts

Unstructured and long abstracts can be hard to read. Over half of the medical literature published in PubMed RTC has unstructured abstracts. Developed a web app that annotates sections (objective, background, methods, results, and conclusion) in such abstracts which makes it skimmable. A Bi-LSTM-Attention model trained with 2 million sentences from 200,000 medical abstracts classifies the and annotates the sentences.
